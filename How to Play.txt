How to Play
To play select a difficulty or if playing 2 player choose "Disable PC" 

You are able to change the color of the ball and paddle.
The difficulties effect the computer's max speed. Or you may choose custom to put a custom value for the computer's max speed.
WARNING!!! Custom Value is unstable over 100
Press F to enable and disable fullscreen once the game started.
